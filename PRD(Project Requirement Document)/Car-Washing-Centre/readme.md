# PRD For TOP NOTCH CAR WASH CENTRE

## OBJECTIVE:

- To show case our work to online
- To give info about our car washing center to online people

## SCOPE (Boundary)

1. to show our shop online
2. to get online booking of car wash
3. to inform people shop open or not
4. to send notification after washing done

## USER STORIES

- I want to book date to wash my car.
- I want to send my car to wash . I am busy person
- I want to contact car wash centre near my area just by one tap

## FEATURES

1.p1

- show name of name of shop , images , equipments and area of car keeping area
- to show time , day, busy, available, slot
- to send sms , notification on customer after car wash
- to keep before and after galleries to build trust
- add interactive map to find location
- to show rating , reviews of user
- to keep record of day, month , year of total car wash , income (stats)

  2.P2

- payment gateway
- accept booking online
